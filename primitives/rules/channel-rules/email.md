# Channel rules — Email

## Sending limits

- New / unwarmed inbox: ≤ 30/day for the first 14 days, then ramp by ~10/day until 200.
- Warm inbox: ≤ 200/day per sending domain.
- Never send the same template to > 80 prospects without an approved variant test.

## Timing

- Don't send before 7am or after 7pm in the recipient's time zone.
- Don't send on weekends unless the play explicitly opts in (rare — usually consumer).

## Content

- One CTA per email.
- Subject line ≤ 7 words, personalized.
- Plain text only. No HTML, no images, no tracking pixels by default (they tank
  deliverability).
- Always include a one-line, real signature with a name and role.

## Compliance

- Honor unsubscribe requests instantly. Save them as `preference` memories so the team
  never re-contacts.
- For US recipients: include a physical address in the footer (CAN-SPAM).
- For EU recipients: pause until you've confirmed the GDPR basis (legitimate interest +
  opt-out trail).

## When NOT to use email

- The buyer has a phone number on file and you have < 200 prospects total. Call instead.
- The previous touch was an unanswered email < 4 days ago.
- You don't have a verified email address.
