# Channel rules — Cold call

## Timing

- 8am – 6pm in the prospect's local time zone, weekdays only.
- Best windows: Tuesday and Wednesday, 10–11:30am and 2–4pm local.
- Don't call the same number more than once per 24h.
- Cap at 6 attempts per prospect over 14 days. After that, switch channels.

## Conversation structure (3 steps)

1. **Build credibility.** 8–12 second intro that names what you do specifically and
   why the prospect makes sense. Lead with a problem hook, not a value prop.
2. **Build value.** One specific outcome you've produced for someone like them, with a
   number. Then a question that confirms the problem is real.
3. **Book the meeting.** Propose a specific time, not "do you have a few minutes?".

## Hard don'ts

- Never read a generic script. The first 12 seconds must be personalized.
- Never argue with an objection — acknowledge it, then redirect with new information.
- Never call after 6pm or before 8am local time.
- Never call a number flagged as Do-Not-Call in memory.
