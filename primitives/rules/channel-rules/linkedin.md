# Channel rules — LinkedIn

## Sending limits

- ≤ 100 connection requests / week per profile.
- ≤ 25 InMails / week.
- Don't message 1st-degree connections more than once per 30 days.

## Content

- Connection request note ≤ 200 characters. No company URL. No CTA.
- First DM after connect: ≤ 4 sentences. Reference one specific thing from their
  profile or recent activity. No meeting ask in message 1.
- Meeting ask is in message 2 at earliest.

## When NOT to use LinkedIn

- The buyer has a verified email AND a real trigger — email is shorter and more
  measurable.
- The buyer has < 500 connections (often inactive).
- It's a Friday or Sunday.
