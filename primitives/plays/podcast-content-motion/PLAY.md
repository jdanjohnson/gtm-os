# Podcast-to-Content Marketing Motion

Transform podcast episodes into a full content marketing motion — blog posts, social clips, newsletter sections, quote graphics, SEO articles.

## Outcome

**Goal:** Extract maximum marketing value from every podcast episode

## Hypothesis

Each podcast episode can generate 15+ content pieces across 5 channels, producing more impressions than the episode itself.

## Success Criteria

- **content_pieces_per_episode**: >15
- **total_impressions**: >10x episode listens across all derived content
- **production_time**: <45 minutes per episode repurposing cycle

## Knowledge Required

- **brand-voice**: Platform-specific tone adaptation rules *(required)*
- **content-calendar**: Publishing schedule for derived content
- **episode-format**: Standard podcast episode structure and recurring segments

## Workflows

- `content-repurposing-pipeline`

## Skills

- `podcast-content-suite`
- `content-repurposer`
- `social-repurposer`
- `seo-keyword-cluster-builder`
- `email-template-generator`

## Tools

- **web-search**: Find trending angles related to episode topics

## Agent Config

- **Role:** Podcast Content Producer
- **Process:** sequential
- **Phases:** design, build, execute, measure, learn

## Experiments

### Clip Format Test

Audio clips vs quote graphics vs short video, measure engagement per platform

### Publish Timing Test

All content same day vs drip over 5 days, measure total reach

### SEO Integration Test

Episode-derived blog post vs standalone SEO article, measure ranking speed
