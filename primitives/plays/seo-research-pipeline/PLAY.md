# SEO Research Pipeline

Full SEO research workflow — SERP analysis, social research, keyword validation, article planning — before any content is written.

## Steps

1. **SERP analysis** → tool: `web-search`
2. **Social research** → tool: `web-search`
   - sources: `['reddit', 'youtube']`
   - count: `5_each`
3. **Keyword validation** → tool: `web-search`
4. **Cluster mapping** → skill: `seo-keyword-cluster-builder`
5. **Article planning** → output: `article_plan`
